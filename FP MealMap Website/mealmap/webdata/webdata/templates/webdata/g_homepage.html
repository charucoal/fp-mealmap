GOOD FOOD DON'T DESERVE A BAD REP.
MEALMAP HELPS WITH THAT.

MealMap is built to tackle food waste while connecting local communities to affordable, quality meals and food items. We help small food businesses minimise surplus and give customers easy access to great food deals nearby.

FOR CUSTOMERS

Discover discounted or free food offers from nearby restaurants, cafes, and stores with just a click. Once a business posts an available food alert, you'll receive a notification if you're within range. You can head over, claim the deal, and enjoy a meal that might otherwise go to waste — simple, affordable, and impactful.

FOR BUSINESSES

Got surplus food or items near expiry? MealMap helps you quickly notify potential customers in your area. Post an alert with your offer, and our system automatically notifies users within increasing distance ranges if food remains unclaimed. This helps reduce waste, recover some costs, and bring new customers through your doors — all while supporting a greener community.
